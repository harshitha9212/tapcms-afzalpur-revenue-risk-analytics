# TAPCMS AFZALPUR LTD. — Revenue Risk Analytics
## NCDC Internship Project

## Project Overview
Predictive financial and risk analytics for agricultural cooperative infrastructure.

## Data
- 5 income streams from TAPCMS Afzalpur
- Year 1 actual revenue data

## Analysis
1. Revenue segmentation
2. Cohort retention modeling
3. Customer lifetime value (CLV)
4. Scenario analysis (0%-71% churn)

## Key Findings
- **71.1% revenue concentration** in fertilizer sales creates significant vulnerability
- **Break-even point**: Project becomes unviable at ~35% overall churn
- **Highest CLV**: Fertilizer (₹175L) despite medium churn risk
- **Most stable**: Rental income (90% retention, low churn)

## Recommendations

| Priority | Action | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Immediate** | Secure 3-year rental contracts | Lock in ₹12.79L stable revenue |
| **Short-term** | Increase PDS/MSP procurement volumes | Reduce fertilizer dependence to &lt;60% |
| **Medium-term** | Diversify into seed/pesticide sales | New revenue stream, same customer base |
| **Ongoing** | Maintain 6-month expense reserve | Buffer against revenue shocks |

## Business Impact

- **Without action**: 20% fertilizer sales drop → PAT falls 20% → DSCR below 2.5x
- **With diversification**: Revenue split 50/30/20 → Sustainable even with 30% churn
